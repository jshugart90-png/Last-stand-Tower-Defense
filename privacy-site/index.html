Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Horseshoe Round Me Gaming ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Last Stand Tower Defense (the "Game"). By downloading or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described below.

01 Information We Collect

The Game does not require a traditional account with an email address or password. Depending on how you play, we may collect or process the following:

  • Gameplay profile data: a display nickname, progress, scores, waves survived, gems, unlocks, missions, and related game statistics.
  • Device identifier: a random device ID used to restore your profile on the same device.
  • Purchase information: when you buy in-app products, purchase receipts or tokens are sent to Apple or Google for validation. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.
  • Gameplay analytics: basic events such as purchases completed and gameplay milestones, used to improve stability and balance.
We do not collect your real name, postal address, or precise location. You choose your in-game nickname.

02 How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • Operate core game features and sync progress when online
  • Validate in-app purchases and prevent fraud
  • Maintain leaderboards and gameplay statistics where enabled
  • Improve performance, stability, and game balance
  • Respond to support requests

03 Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play: in-app purchase processing and receipt validation.
  • Cloud hosting and database providers: secure storage of gameplay profiles when you play online.

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies. We share only what is necessary to operate the Game.

The current version of Last Stand Tower Defense does not include third-party advertising SDKs (such as AdMob).

04 Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience consistent with its age rating on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

05 Data Retention & Deletion

Gameplay data is retained while your profile remains active so we can provide game features. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below. Local-only progress stored only on your device can be reset from in-game settings.

06 Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect gameplay data stored on our servers. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard information against unauthorized access.

07 Your Choices

  • You may play without providing an email address.
  • You may reset local progress from the in-game Settings screen.
  • You may contact us to request access to or deletion of server-stored gameplay data associated with your profile.
  • In-app purchases are optional and processed by Apple or Google under their respective terms.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Game after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
