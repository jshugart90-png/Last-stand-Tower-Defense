Privacy Policy

Last Stand Tower Defense (the “App”) is operated by John Shugart. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Gameplay data: nickname, progress, scores, waves survived, gems, unlocks, and in-app purchase records needed to run the game.
  • Device identifier: a random device ID used to restore your profile on the same device.
  • Analytics events: basic gameplay events (for example purchases and ad reward claims) to improve the game.
  • Purchase receipts: when you buy in-app products, receipt data is sent to Apple or Google for validation. We do not store full payment card details.

How we use information

We use collected data to operate game features, sync progress, validate purchases, prevent fraud, and improve stability.

Third-party services

  • Apple App Store / Google Play: in-app purchase processing and receipt validation.
  • Hosting & database providers: secure storage of gameplay profiles.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data retention & deletion

Gameplay data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting support.
